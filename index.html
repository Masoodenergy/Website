OUR STORY

Founded 2014, Ottawa, Canada

We began our journey with repurposing used electric vehicle batteries for grid level storage. Electric vehicle batteries retain 80% of their initial capacity intact at the end of automotive use. These batteries still retain significant storage capacity, but do not meet the requirements for automotive use. Used EV batteries can provide an estimated 10 years of additional storage. 

The first step to repurposing used electric vehicle batteries is predicting battery life. Through years of research and development to solve the problem of predicting battery life, it led us to develop AI capabilities. AI provided us the ability to tackle a whole new set of problems to meet the challenges of tomorrow.
